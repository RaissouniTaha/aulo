About Our Agency

The Government Urban Agency is responsible for implementing the national urban planning policy, developing urban plans, and regulating construction and development activities across the country.

Our mission is to ensure sustainable urban development, improve the quality of life in our cities, and preserve our architectural and cultural heritage.
